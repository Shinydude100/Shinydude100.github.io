What: Added the `defer` attribute to the `<script>` tags for `memory-backplane.js` and `script.js` in `index.html`.

Why: Loading JavaScript files synchronously, even at the end of the `<body>`, can delay the parsing and rendering of HTML if the scripts are large. This resolves a severe "Render-blocking requests" penalty on mobile devices that was delaying the Largest Contentful Paint (LCP).

Impact: Improved Largest Contentful Paint on mobile by removing render-blocking script requests, ensuring the browser can render the page without waiting for scripts to download and execute.

Measurement: Verified with local Playwright E2E testing to ensure adding `defer` introduced no regressions to application logic or DOM load sequencing.
