🎨 Palette: Improve footer location text color contrast

**What:** Increased the opacity of the `.footer-loc` text color in `index.html` from `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.15)` to `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.5)`.

**Why:** The previous color contrast ratio against the footer background (`rgba(9, 11, 16, 0.95)`) was roughly 1.48:1, failing WCAG 4.5:1 accessibility standards. The new opacity provides a contrast ratio of roughly 5.3:1, making the text accessible.

**Before/After:**
*   **Before:** `.footer-loc` color was `rgba(255,255,255,0.15)`.
*   **After:** `.footer-loc` color is `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.5)`.

**Accessibility:** Improves text legibility for users with visual impairments, ensuring compliance with WCAG contrast requirements.
